This application is a Check List for Students travelling to the United States to pursue their Higher Education. It is specially designed for Indian Students, keeping in mind their specific requirements. Also, it is a result of personal experience and after going through several websites for a list of things to pack I came up with this application. As simple and elegant as it looks, it's equally useful for those last minute ambivalent feelings before leaving your home. Amidst such times, this app will help you make sure that you have the minimum required things to start a new life in a new country!
